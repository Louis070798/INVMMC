# Lo trinh trien khai

## Phase 0 - Foundation, 1 tuan

- Chot danh muc du an, ngan sach, vai tro.
- Chot ma chuyen khoan va ma request.
- Tao repo, CI, env, database.
- Tao Telegram bot sandbox.

## Phase 1 - MVP kiem soat chi, 2-3 tuan

- Tao de nghi chi qua Telegram/API.
- Approval engine theo han muc.
- Budget check co ban.
- Audit log.
- Bao cao danh sach de nghi theo trang thai.

## Phase 2 - Doi soat, 2-4 tuan

- Import sao ke CSV/Excel hoac webhook VietQR/Casso.
- Matching theo ma noi dung, so tien, thoi gian.
- Man hinh xu ly giao dich chua khop.
- Canh bao Telegram.

## Phase 3 - MoMo/ngan hang production, 4-8 tuan

- Ky hop dong/API provider.
- Implement signature verification.
- Kiem thu sandbox.
- Pen test webhook va quyen truy cap.
- Chay song song voi quy trinh ke toan hien tai.

## Phase 4 - AI local, 2-4 tuan

- Trich xuat du lieu tu chat/hoa don.
- Goi y budget line.
- Duplicate detection.
- Executive summary theo du an.

## Phase 5 - Scale 100 nguoi

- SSO.
- Dashboard web.
- Worker queue.
- Backup/restore.
- Monitoring.
- Phan quyen nang cao theo phong ban va du an.
